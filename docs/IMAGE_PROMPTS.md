# Image Prompts for Wonder Nails

Use these prompts with Midjourney (v6) or DALL-E 3 to generate high-quality assets for the hero carousel.

## General Style
> **Style:** High-end beauty photography, macro shot, studio lighting, 8k resolution, hyper-realistic, elegant, premium aesthetic.
> **Colors:** Vibrant pinks (#FF2D6A) and purples (#B025FF), white background or soft gradient.

## Slide Prompts

### 1. Manicure Premium (Ruby Red)
**Concept:** Elegant hand with glossy ruby red gel nails.
**Prompt:**
```
Close-up macro shot of a woman's hand with perfect ruby red gel manicure, holding a delicate crystal or flower, high-end beauty photography, studio lighting, soft pink background, glossy finish, 8k --ar 4:5 --v 6.0
```
